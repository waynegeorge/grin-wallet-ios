Grin Wallet

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 17, 2026

Grin Wallet does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Your keys never leave your device. This is a testnet wallet — all transactions use testnet Grin, which has no monetary value.

Overview

Grin Wallet is a non-custodial, self-sovereign testnet wallet for the Grin cryptocurrency. It connects exclusively to the Grin testnet — all Grin transacted within the app has no real-world monetary value. "Non-custodial" means the developer has no access to your private keys, seed phrase, or transaction history at any time.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, process, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Private keys or seed phrases
  • Wallet balances or transaction history
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Financial information of any kind

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All wallet data — including your encrypted seed phrase, private keys, and transaction history — is stored exclusively on your device using iOS secure storage (Keychain and the app's sandboxed local directory). This data is never uploaded to any server controlled by the developer.

Network Connections

Grin Wallet connects to Grin network nodes solely to broadcast and retrieve transactions on the Grin blockchain. These connections are made directly from your device and are necessary for the app to function. No identifying information about you is included in these communications.

The app also supports peer-to-peer transaction exchange via Bluetooth (MultipeerConnectivity). This communication occurs directly between devices and is not routed through any server.

No Third-Party Services

Grin Wallet does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with third parties.

No Account Required

Grin Wallet does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app.

Children's Privacy

Grin Wallet does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected, this app complies with COPPA and equivalent regulations by design.

Your Responsibility

Because Grin Wallet is non-custodial, you are solely responsible for safeguarding your seed phrase and device. As this is a testnet wallet, all Grin transacted has no monetary value — however, your seed phrase should still be kept private as good security practice.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
